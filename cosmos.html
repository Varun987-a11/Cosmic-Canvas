Dark Matter

Structure Formation

From fluctuations to galaxies

Tiny quantum fluctuations laid down during inflation grew under gravity. Over billions of years they formed the web of cosmic structure we see today.

Dark matter halos

Halos of dark matter gather gas; baryons cool, fragment and form stars. Simulations reproduce galaxy populations well when dark matter is included.

Galaxies & clusters

Black Holes & Exoplanets

Black holes

Objects where gravity traps light. Characterised by mass and spin. Observational breakthroughs: gravitational waves and horizon-scale imaging.

Exoplanets

Thousands discovered; methods include transits, radial velocity and imaging. JWST provides atmospheric spectra for characterisation.
