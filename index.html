The Universe is Mostly Invisible

The stars, planets, and galaxies we see make up a **shockingly small $\sim 5\%$** of the universe's total mass-energy content. The vast majority is composed of mysterious dark matter and dark energy.

  • **Dark Energy** ($68\%$): Accelerates cosmic expansion.
  • **Dark Matter** ($27\%$): Provides gravitational pull for galaxies.
  • **Normal Matter** ($5\%$): Everything we can see and touch.
